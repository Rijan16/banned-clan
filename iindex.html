Planets of the Solar System

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in the Solar System.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is Earth's closest planetary neighbor.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the Red Planet.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its stunning rings.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and has a unique blue color.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in the Solar System.
